Brand Identity System

Complete visual identity for a tech startup focusing on sustainable solutions

Project Overview

We're building a complete brand identity system for GreenTech Solutions, an innovative startup that's developing sustainable technology for urban environments. This project involves creating everything from the core logo and visual identity to comprehensive brand guidelines that will establish their presence in the competitive clean-tech market.

The brand needs to communicate innovation, sustainability, and trustworthiness while appealing to both B2B clients and environmentally conscious consumers. We're looking for talented designers who can help bring this vision to life through thoughtful design decisions and collaborative teamwork.

What We're Building

  • Primary logo and brand mark variations
  • Complete color palette and typography system
  • Business card and stationery designs
  • Website mockups and UI elements
  • Brand guidelines document
  • Social media templates

What We're Looking For

  • Graphic Designer – Experience with logo design and brand identity systems. Proficiency in Adobe Creative Suite (Illustrator, Photoshop, InDesign).
  • UI/UX Designer – Knowledge of web design principles and experience creating cohesive digital brand experiences. Figma or Sketch experience preferred.
  • Brand Strategist – Understanding of market positioning and brand development. Experience with brand guidelines and style guide creation.
